About StudyGroups

With this web you can share the study time with other students, meeting at the library or at free classrooms in the university. This app is thought to help students that don't want to study alone and create a meeting point for students in your university. You can both create a group, and wait for other students to join you, or you can directly join groups other students have created before. Hope you will enjoy!
