About us

We are a group of young students from UPC, and we have participated in HackUPC this year 2018. Our project consist in creating a web-application that allows its users to find and create study-groups. You can meet with other students who wants to study the same subject as you in the library or in free classrooms in our campus. We don't want you to study alone, so we have learned HTML and PHP to develop a web that will help you to find study-mates. We hope you will enjoy our demo as we have enjoyed this experience.
